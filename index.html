🎯
Our Mission

RESPONSIBLE RECYCLING FOR ALL

India generated 13.97 lakh tonnes of e-waste in FY 2024–25 alone (CPCB). We bridge the gap between e-waste generators and certified recyclers — ensuring every discarded device is handled responsibly, reducing toxic landfill contamination and recovering precious materials back into the supply chain.

🔭
Our Vision

ZERO E-WASTE TO LANDFILL

NITI Aayog projects India's e-waste will hit 14 million tonnes by 2030. Through technology, community engagement, and certified recycling partnerships, we're racing to make zero-landfill a reality — one doorstep pickup at a time.
